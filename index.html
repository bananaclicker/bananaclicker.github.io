PATCH NOTES

3/2/22 @ 2:25 PM

- Bundles are fixed and have been reworked - Bundles can give you the amount of each item you have divided by a random number between 25 and 50. - The price value you get on the bundle is random between 1.5 and 3.5. - Bundles now show up every 2 minutes instead of 1.5 - Getting free items from the bundle also increases the price of that item just as if you were to buy it normally.

3/2/22 @ 1:45 PM

- Several bug fixes
- Bundles have been temporarily disabled until they are fixed (For some reason they stopped working)
- Achievements now have a fixed position on the screen, no matter if you are scrolled down.

3/1/22 @ 9:45 PM

- Save Feature: save files are now in a complicated format to avoid cheating
- Battles: slight rework & bug fixes
- Help (top left button) has been updated

2/20/22 @ 11:25 AM

- Many glitches related to battles have been fixed
- Other small changes

2/19/22 @ 7:45 PM

- New feature: Battles
- Unlocked at 100K bananas
- Battle AI opponents with an aim-trainer-like game and bet shop items to win or lose them.
- Many bug fixes, including buttons at the top of the screen having an inaccurate hitbox (fixed)

2/18/22 @ 5:00 PM

- Bundles have been reworked, many glitches are fixed

2/17/22 @ 10:00 PM

- New shop item: Bundles
- Bundles become available every 1.5 minutes, they are value packs with items and prices depending on the items you currently have. Lots of randomness is involved.

2/16/22 @ 2:00 PM

- New Saving feature now also saves color mode (dark/light) and difficulty.

2/15/22 @ 8:45 PM

- New feature: Saving
- Game can now be locally saved in a file that can be uploaded to resume progress.

2/15/22 @ 4:00 PM

- Fixed menus overlapping (glitch)
- Dark & Light Mode added (in settings)
- Custom Scrollbar added
- Achievement Rewards Balancing
- Other small changes

2/14/22 @ 7:15 PM

- Fixed a major bug where clicks weren't registering

2/14/22 @ 5:00 PM

- Fixed some bugs related to Achievements
- Multiple Achievements can now be displayed at once
